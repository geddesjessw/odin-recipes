Taco Recipe

An easy crowd pleaser sure to satisfy the whole family.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Avacado
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Peppers
  • Lettuce
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in medium sized skillet.
  2. Add taco seasoning of choice.
  3. Once beef is browned, drain grease and set beef aside in a serving dish.
  4. Sautee onions (and peppers if used). Chop lettuce and tomatoes, slice avacadoes, grate cheese. Set aside in serving dishes.
  5. Once veggies have been sauteed, begin warming tortillas on a cast iron pan.

To serve, add ground beef to tortilla. Next add toppings such as cheese, sauteed vegetables, tomatoes and sour creme. Serve with optional salsa.
